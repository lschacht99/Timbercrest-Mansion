What to do around

Four seasons around Mount Snow

World-class skiing in winter, lakes and biking in summer, and some of Vermont's best leaf-peeping each fall — all minutes from your private mansion.

winter

Mount Snow Resort

600 acres, 20 lifts, 1,900 ft vertical across four faces. 5 min from the mansions.

winter

Carinthia Parks

Mount Snow's dedicated freestyle terrain-park face — jumps, rails and pipes.

winter

Snowmobile & dog sledding

Guided snowmobile tours, cross-country trails, snowshoeing and dog sledding.

winter

Snow tubing

Lift-served tubing lanes — the easiest big laughs for non-skiers.

fall

Bluebird Express foliage rides

Scenic chairlift rides with views to Somerset Reservoir. Peak color: first ten days of October.

fall

Mt. Olga fire tower

A short family hike to a fire tower with a 360° view of the foliage.

summer

Valley Trail

A gentle walking and biking path linking West Dover to Wilmington.

summer

Lake & summer adventure

Nearby lakes for swimming and paddling, plus mountain biking, golf and hiking.

all

Adams Family Farm

A working Vermont farm — animals, wagon and sleigh rides, and maple everything.

all

Wilmington village

Historic village 10 minutes away — shops, galleries, breweries and restaurants.

all

Dot's Restaurant

A classic American diner with a retro vibe — the local pick for any meal.

Getting here

All mansions sit in Dover & West Dover, Vermont — just off scenic Route 100, minutes from the Mount Snow base. Exact address and door codes are shared after booking.
